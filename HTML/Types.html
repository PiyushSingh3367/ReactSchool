What is Supply Side Platforms


A supply-side platform is a piece of software used to sell advertising in an automated fashion. SSPs are most often used by online publishers to help them sell display, video and mobile ads.

The Supply Side Platform maximizes revenue across all screens and ad formats by exposing each impression to all demand sources simultaneously in one comprehensive auction. For publishers, this means increased competition between network and real-time buyers (RTB). All of this is made available in a platform that incorporates extensive reporting, ad quality controls, yield management and private marketplace deals

SSPs allow publishers to connect their inventory to multiple ad exchanges, DSPs, and networks at once.
